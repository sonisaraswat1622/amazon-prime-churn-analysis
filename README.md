# amazon-prime-churn-analysis
Amazon Prime Video lost ~38% of its Q1 2024 churns with zero behavioral trace after the ad-tier launch. I diagnosed why their blended churn KPI is structurally misleading, segmented 258M subscribers into 5 risk cohorts, and proposed a confidence-weighted metric (CSWC) to surface the hidden gap. Built with Python, SQL &amp; Power BI.
